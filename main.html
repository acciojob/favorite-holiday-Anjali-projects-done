Paris - The City of Lights


Paris, the capital of France, is known for its rich history, stunning architecture, and world-class cuisine. It is a dream destination for travelers who love culture, fashion, and art.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower - The iconic landmark of Paris, offering breathtaking views of the city.
  2. Louvre Museum - Home to the world-famous Mona Lisa and countless other masterpieces.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - A stunning example of Gothic architecture with beautiful stained glass windows.
  4. Champs-Élysées - A vibrant avenue filled with shops, cafes, and theaters.
  5. Sacré-Cœur Basilica - A stunning white basilica located on Montmartre, offering panoramic city views.

Must-Try Foods in Paris

  • Croissants - Buttery, flaky, and perfect with a cup of coffee.
  • Macarons - Colorful and delicate French pastries with a sweet filling.
  • Escargots - A delicacy made of snails cooked with garlic and butter.
  • French Onion Soup - A rich, flavorful soup topped with melted cheese.
  • Crème Brûlée - A creamy dessert with a caramelized sugar top.
